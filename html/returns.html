RETURNS POLICY & INSTRUCTIONS

  • -XEXYMIX will gladly accept unworn, unwashed products with original tags for return within 7 days from the date on which you received the package
  • -Please make sure to contact with XEXYMIX CS team first before sending the package.
  • -All top item with the enclosed bra pads should be come with Bra top for refund.
  • -We don’t accept the Exchange / Return for Inner wears and cosmetic due to hygienic reasons
  • -XEXYMIX is not responsible for any lost returns.
  • -We currently do not generate return labels for international shipments, customers will be responsible for return shipments & cost.
  • -If [ Total Price - Price of Returning Items ] is below free shipping amount, 20.00 USD will be deducted from your refund amount. In case of pre-paid shipping fees there shall not be any deductions in your refund amount.

For order returns, please follow the shipping instructions carefully.
For further inquires please send us an email to XEXYMIX Global Customer Service at xexycs@brandxcorp.com
